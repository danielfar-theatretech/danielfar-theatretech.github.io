My Current Arts Practice

This is an outline of my main art form specialism. Including evidence of my own experience in this arts area.

Currently, I work and volunteer as a theatre technician. I specialise in Lighting Design and Production but as I generally work in smaller venues, I often end up working on light, sound, and projection.

My experiences range from village halls, seating 50, to the Mercury Theatre Colchester, seating over 500!

Projects and works include pantomimes, serious dramas, contemporary works, musicals, immersive experiences and more.
